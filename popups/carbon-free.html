Ecoworks Foundation has sponsored Carbon-Free Prosperity 2025: How the Northwest Can Create Jobs, Enable Energy Independence, and Be a Global Clean-Energy Leader. This groundbreaking report details an assesment and vision for Oregon, Washington and British Columbia as prepared by Clean Edge, Inc. and Climate Solutions, two environmental leaders in the region.

Carbon-Free Prosperity 2025 provides valuable guidance to venture capital firms, industry leaders, entrepreneurs and government officials as they each address clean-energy needs. The report aims to:

  • Assess the Northwest's current clean-energy assets;
  • Illuminate pathways to a vibrant clean-energy industry cluster that creates tens of thousands of jobs; and
  • Highlight how the region could be one of the first in the world to reach carbon neutrality by deploying a strategic mix of clean-energy technologies.
